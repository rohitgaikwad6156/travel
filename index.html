Why are these 3 fields required?

TripMate sends emails directly from your browser — there's no server. It uses EmailJS (a free service) to do this. EmailJS needs 3 identifiers to know who you are, which email account to send from, and what the email should look like.

1
Public Key — Your EmailJS account ID. A random string like abc123XYZ.
📍 Find at: emailjs.com → Account → General → Public Key
⚠️ This is NOT your email address.
2
Service ID — Which email account to send from (e.g. your Gmail). Always starts with service_.
📍 Find at: emailjs.com → Email Services → click your service
3
Template ID — The email layout/template you designed. Always starts with template_.
📍 Find at: emailjs.com → Email Templates → click your template
First time? 3-minute setup:
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account
  2. Click Email Services → Add New Service → choose Gmail → connect your Gmail
  3. Click Email Templates → Create New Template → add these variables in the body: {{to_name}} {{to_email}} {{trip_plan}} {{destination}}
  4. Copy the 3 IDs below and click Save Credentials
